															DISPATCH
This tool is only available during emergencies.  It allows you to direct your police and firefighters to suppress problems.

Dispatch Police - Police are useful for suppressing riots and resisting floods.

Dispatch Firefighters- Firefighters suppress fire and resist floods and toxic clouds.

Dispatch Military - Military units may be available to your city after the military has constructed a base. Military units are highly trained and are effective against a variety of disasters.
